Step 1 of 3: Is this an observational study or a controlled experiment?

Asnwer: This is a controlled experiment

Step 2 of 3: Identify the response and explanatory variables.

Answer:
- Response variable; Bounce rate
- Explanatory variable: Budget Strategy

Step 3 of 3: Identify the control group and the experimental group.

Answer: 
- Control group: Original budget strategy (50/50)
- Treatment group: New budget strategy (25/75)
